Our Story

About Gupshup

At Gupshup, we believe that great food is best enjoyed in great company. Our name, which means "chit-chat" in Hindi, reflects our commitment to creating a warm, inviting space where friends and families can gather, share stories, and create memories.

Founded in the heart of Airoli, Navi Mumbai, we bring together traditional Indian recipes passed down through generations with contemporary cooking techniques. Every dish is prepared with love, using the finest ingredients and authentic spices.
